# Assessment-as-a-Service (B2B) Sales Playbook

## Goal
Convert early pilots into paid subscriptions by focusing on measurable hiring outcomes, speed, and quality of signal.

## Target Buyers
- CTOs and Engineering Managers at small-to-mid tech companies
- Heads of Talent/HR in growth-stage startups

## Core Pitch
"We help teams screen engineers faster with a structured coding assessment + AI interview, producing a clear candidate report so you can move forward with confidence."

## Qualification (Quick Checklist)
- Hiring 2+ engineers in the next 90 days
- Screening time > 3 days per candidate
- Inconsistent or subjective evaluation process

## Pilot Offer (Default)
- 5 candidates assessed in 7 days
- Flat $299 pilot fee (waived if they convert to paid plan)
- Report delivered within 24 hours of completion

## Objection Handling
### "We already use take-home tests"
- Our reports add structured interview insights and consistent scoring.

### "We need something enterprise-grade"
- We can pilot on a small batch and expand with SLA + SSO.

### "We’re worried about candidate experience"
- The flow is short, transparent, and includes a clear completion timeline.

## Key Assets to Prepare
- 2-minute product demo video
- One-page PDF of candidate report
- Pricing page with plan comparison
- Short case study (even if it’s a pilot)

## Conversion Path
1. 15-minute discovery call
2. 7-day pilot with 5 candidates
3. Review report quality and completion metrics
4. Convert to Starter or Growth plan

## KPIs
- Pilot-to-paid conversion rate > 30%
- Average time to report < 24h
- Completion rate > 60%
